To install Linux Imaginary follow the installation guide:

-1 |Open the live iso
   |
0  |Connect to the internet
0.1|systemctl start NetworkManager
0.2|nmtui
   |
1  |Go into the installer and add execute permissions
1.1|cd LinuxImaginaryInstaller
1.2|chmod +x install.sh
   |
2  |Launch the installer!
2.1|./install.sh

Your new system will be ready soon
